About Athr

Athr was founded with a clear mission: to provide organizations with actionable threat intelligence that is both timely and easy to understand. In a digital landscape filled with noise, we cut through the clutter to deliver alerts that matter. We believe that proactive security shouldn't be a luxury reserved for the few, but a standard accessible to all.

Our value proposition is simple. We monitor a vast array of sources, from the dark web to public code repositories, for any mention of your organization's assets. When a potential threat is detected—be it a credential leak, a vulnerable piece of software, or chatter from a known threat actor—we don't just notify you; we provide you with the context and actionable steps needed to mitigate the risk immediately. This focus on "actionable" intelligence is what sets us apart.

Key features of the Athr platform include our secure search capabilities, which allow you to instantly query our database of monitored sources; live alerts that are prioritized based on severity; and visual analytics that help you understand your organization's threat landscape over time. With Athr, you can move from a reactive to a proactive security posture, stopping threats before they can impact your business.
